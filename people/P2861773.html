Application ideas

  • journey planner: plan out journeys of planes or buses and the site will connect the routes to get you to the destination and it will output a total. Users are: commuters and travellers
  • income tracker: track wages and profit from savings accounts and the money can be converted into the equivalent of other currencies. Users are: people who need to track multiple streams of income, people who go to other countries and people wo are trying to save money
  • roulette generator: spin the wheel with a specific spread, with selected odds or win percentage and it will create random bets to put on to match the criteria. Users are: gamblers and people who are new to gambling
