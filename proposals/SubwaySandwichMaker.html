Subway sandwich Maker

Proposed by: Kane Gent

Brief Description

The idea is simple. A website where you can pick from all the ingrediants that subway have to offer. then the output is all the macros and the KCAL of the sandwich.

people will be able to compare what their best sandwiches are and maybe even compete with friends to make the healthiest or unhelthiest sandwich available.

It needs some sort of database or index that holds all the values of each elements KCAL and Macros. then a math system that adds all elemetns and assignes them to a made sandwich at the end.
